# Curriculum Overview

A four-day intensive for senior Angular engineers. We go beyond syntax into the architectural decisions that make large Angular codebases fast, reactive, and maintainable. Hands-on labs, real code from your codebase, cohort capped at 12.

# Custom RxJS operators & reactive patterns

RxJS is the substrate of every serious Angular app. Learn to think in streams — higher-order mapping, multicasting pitfalls, building your own typed operators, and cancellation that actually works. Refactor tangled subscribe-heavy services into single declarative pipelines.

# Zone-less signal architecture

Signals are the biggest shift in Angular since standalone components. Rebuild a feature end-to-end with `signal`, `computed`, and `effect` — no `zone.js`, no manual change detection — plus a migration playbook for existing zone-based codebases.

# Enterprise schematics & monorepo scaling

Once you cross five squads, ad-hoc folder structures stop working. Nx workspace layout, module boundary enforcement, custom schematics and generators, affected graphs, and CI partitioning that scales Angular to dozens of teams sharing one repo.

# Performance profiling & hydration strategy

Profile a real app and remove the top three bottlenecks live. Chrome DevTools traces, the Angular profiler, SSR with `@defer` partial hydration, bundle budgets, and real-user Core Web Vitals you can actually alert on.
